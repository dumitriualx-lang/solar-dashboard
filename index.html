❓ How this app works

This dashboard estimates your solar panel production in real time using weather data from Open-Meteo and your system configuration. It combines live solar irradiance, panel tilt, temperature derating and your inverter efficiency to calculate AC output.

⚡ Solar production

Calculated from Direct Normal Irradiance (DNI), Diffuse Horizontal Irradiance (DHI) and your panel angle (Plane of Array model). Temperature derating is applied using NOCT 45°C and a −0.37%/°C coefficient. Inverter efficiency is configured in your system settings.

⚠️ Consumption — fixed daily profile

Home consumption uses a fixed daily profile you configure once. The app auto-fills the correct kW value based on the current hour.

📋 Log tab

The Log tab shows your cumulative energy summary — produced, consumed, self-sufficiency and daily average — calculated automatically from all recorded snapshots.

🔋 Battery

The battery SOC you enter maps to the usable range: 10% = empty (reserve floor), 90% = full (charge ceiling). Enter the SOC shown in your solar panel application.

📅 Forecast

7-day hourly forecast uses Open-Meteo weather data. Daily yield is estimated from cloud cover and irradiance forecast.

🔔 Notifications

Smart alerts check every 15 minutes and send one combined notification.

📡 Data sources

Weather & irradiance: Open-Meteo (free, no API key, updates every 5 min).
Location: device GPS.
All calculations run locally on your device — no data is sent anywhere.

🚀 App updates

The app checks for updates automatically on launch.
